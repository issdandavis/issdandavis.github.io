Frequently Asked Questions

What is SCBE-AETHERMOORE?

SCBE-AETHERMOORE is an AI safety and governance framework that uses hyperbolic geometry (the Poincare ball model) to create a 14-layer security pipeline for AI agent fleets. It makes adversarial behavior mathematically infeasible by exponentially scaling the cost of unauthorized actions. The framework includes post-quantum cryptography (ML-KEM-768 and ML-DSA-65), a Sacred Tongues tokenizer with golden-ratio weighted semantic domains, and full fleet management for autonomous AI agents.

How does hyperbolic geometry prevent AI attacks?

In hyperbolic space, distance from the safe center grows exponentially as you approach the boundary. SCBE maps all AI agent actions into this space using the cost function H(d, R) = R^(d^2). Safe, authorized operations stay near the center where cost is low. Adversarial behavior drifts toward the boundary where the cost becomes computationally infeasible. This is a mathematical guarantee, not just a policy rule.

What is the EU AI Act and how does SCBE help with compliance?

The EU AI Act is comprehensive regulation for artificial intelligence systems, with enforcement beginning August 2, 2026. Penalties for non-compliance can reach 35 million EUR. SCBE provides the governance audit trail, risk classification (ALLOW / QUARANTINE / ESCALATE / DENY), and cryptographic attestation that AI systems need to demonstrate compliance. Every governance decision is logged with post-quantum signed timestamps.
