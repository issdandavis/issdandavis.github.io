Buyer Manual

AI Governance Toolkit

This package is for teams that need a practical governance layer before they need the full SCBE architecture. It should help you make safer decisions, document them clearly, and start with a small controlled pilot instead of a giant rollout.

Decision records Threshold worksheets Pilot-first AI-readable manual

First-run path

1. UnpackOpen the files from the delivery bundle and keep them in one working folder.
2. Choose one workflowPick a single real automation or AI decision point to govern first.
3. Set thresholdsFill the worksheet with your starting allow, quarantine, and deny boundaries.
4. Record a decisionUse the decision record template on one concrete example.
5. ReviewCheck whether the chosen thresholds match the operational reality you saw.

Who this package is for
